❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How does the disease detection work? ▼
TomatoGuard uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of tomato leaf images. Upload a clear photo of a tomato leaf, and the AI analyzes it to detect diseases with up to 93% accuracy.
How many free scans do I get? ▼
You get 10 free scans during your 7-day trial period. After that, you can subscribe to Starter (50 scans/month) or Pro (unlimited scans) plans.
How do I subscribe? ▼
Go to the Payment section in the menu, choose your plan, and complete the M-Pesa payment. Your subscription will be activated immediately.
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to Settings → Security → Forgot Password. Enter your email and we'll send you a password reset link.
What if the app can't detect my leaf? ▼
Make sure you upload a clear, well-lit photo of a single tomato leaf. The leaf should fill most of the frame. Avoid blurry, dark, or multiple-leaf images.
